# Smart Municipal Hospital Leave Management System (SMHLMS)

## üìñ Overview
SMHLMS is a **digital leave management system** designed for Salaga Municipal Hospital.  
It aligns with **Ghana Health Service protocols** and sector governance, ensuring transparency, fairness, and compliance in staff leave administration.

The system empowers nurses, managers, HR, and leadership with intuitive dashboards, automated workflows, and audit-ready reporting.

---

## ‚ú® Key Features

### Dashboards
- **Nurses:** View leave balances, apply for leave, track application status  
- **Managers:** Approve/reject requests, monitor staff coverage and skill mix  
- **HR:** Manage entitlement engines, detect conflicts, generate compliance reports  
- **Leadership:** Access audit trails, policy compliance dashboards, and strategic insights  

### Approval Chains
- Multi-level workflows mapped to Ghana Health Service rules  
- Escalation logic for unresolved or overdue requests  

### Entitlement Engine
- Supports annual leave, study leave, maternity/paternity leave, and special leave categories  
- Automatic calculations based on staff category, tenure, and policy rules  

### Conflict Detection
- Prevents overlapping leave requests  
- Ensures minimum staffing levels and balanced skill mix  

### Audit & Compliance
- Full audit trail of requests and approvals  
- Exportable reports for HR and leadership oversight  

### Notifications
- Email/SMS templates for approvals, rejections, and reminders  
- Configurable alerts for managers and HR
